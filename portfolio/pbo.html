Through work as a student officer in Purdue Bands & Orchestras, I and David Teter worked with the PR staff of Purdue Bands and the Purdue office of Media & Marketing to duplicate the Purdue 2014 Cascade theme in WordPress. Developed in Cascade, Media & Marketing did not offer support for departments who have their sites hosted in WordPress instead. We worked to rectify this situation and develop a Purdue 2014 theme within WordPress that allowed support for those not wanting to use Cascade.

We also used Advanced Custom Fields to customize the WordPress interface for Purdue Bands & Orchetras. The staff in charge of updating the site had no coding or development background, so we created a custom edit screen for each WordPress page template that made updating content as hassle-free and self explanatory as possible.

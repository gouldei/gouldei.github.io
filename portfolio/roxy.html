Already an employee of the theater since Spring 2012, I ventured into their employ as a web designer in Spring 2014. I setup a fresh WordPress install for the theater and worked to design them a site that fit their employee and customer needs, but also offered an aesthetic appeal that would present themselves as more than just a small-time business. I based their design on the Motif theme by WordPress.com, but found the site needed more customization than Motif could offer with its built-in utilities. After setting up a child theme of Motif, I made layout and color changes to suit the theater.

The site also featured WordPress customization via Advanced Custom Fields. The Fields were implemented on the home page to allow the management of the theater an easy way to show high-priority news, such as sales or emergency hours.
